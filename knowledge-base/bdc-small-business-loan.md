## 2. LOAN STRUCTURE — TWO TIERS

BDC's Small Business Loan offers two tiers based on loan amount:

| Feature | Faster Approval (up to $100K) | Flexible Terms ($100K–$350K) |
|---------|-------------------------------|------------------------------|
| **Approval time** | Less than 10 days | Less than 30 days |
| **Application fees** | No application or prepayment fees | Certain fees apply |
| **Documentation** | Personal ID only | Personal ID + financial statements for last 2 years |
| **Interest-only payments** | Up to 6 months | Up to 12 months |
| **Amortization period** | 5 years | Up to 8 years |

*Conditions apply. Subject to approval and the timely completion of your application, including providing all relevant information.

### Loan amount:
- **Maximum:** $350,000
- The amount approved is based on BDC's analysis of your request
- Need more than $350K? BDC connects you with their experts for tailored solutions

- Canadian-based: Business registered in Canada

## 4. INTEREST RATES

BDC does NOT publish specific interest rates. The rate is calculated as:

**Current floating base rate + variance based on your personal and business information = interest rate**

As a result, the interest rate varies by client. BDC's approach focuses on more than just the rate — they provide flexible repayment terms, higher levels of financing, and tailored solutions.

## 7. APPLICATION PROCESS

### 3-step online application:

1. **Check eligibility** — In just a few clicks, check if your business is eligible to apply for a business loan. No credit check at this stage.
2. **Apply online** — BDC asks for information about you, your business, its leadership, and shareholders.
3. **Get funds in a few days** — After approval, your funds could be available in less than a week.

### Information required for the online application:

**Business information:**
- Complete and up-to-date business information as listed in your business registry (having a copy of your up-to-date business registry may be helpful)
- Names, email addresses, home address and mobile phone numbers for the company's president, members of the board of directors, and shareholders
- Business void cheque

**Personal information:**
- One piece of government-issued photo ID
- The online application form lets you save your work at various points in case you need to obtain certain details and return later to complete the form

### Approval timeline:
- Depends on number of factors, including how many loan requests BDC receives and how quickly you provide all necessary information
- For loans under $100,000: fewer documents required, process generally faster (less than 10 days)
- For loans $100K–$350K: less than 30 days with complete application

## 8. FAQ (from product page)

### Q: How long does it take to get a loan?
This depends on a number of factors, including how many loan requests BDC receives and how quickly you provide all the necessary information. BDC processes loan requests as quickly as possible. If your loan request is under $100,000, fewer documents are required and the process is generally faster.

### Q: How do you calculate the interest rate and is it the same rate for all clients?
BDC calculates the interest rate on their small business loan as follows: Current floating base rate + variance based on your personal and business information = interest rate. As a result, the interest rate varies by client.

### Q: What type of information will I need to complete the online loan application form?
**Business information:** Complete and up-to-date business information as listed in your business registry, names/email addresses/home address/mobile phone numbers for the company's president, members of the board of directors, and shareholders, business void cheque.
**Personal information:** One piece of government-issued photo ID.

### Q: How much can I borrow?
You can ask for up to $350,000 for a BDC Small Business Loan. The amount approved will be based on BDC's analysis of your request.
